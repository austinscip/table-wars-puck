## ðŸŽ¨ QUESTION STYLE ANALYSIS

### What Makes These YDKJ-Style?

1. **Scenario-Based Setup** - Puts you IN the situation
   - "Your dad stepped on a rusty nail..."
   - "You're at a party and someone dares you..."
   - "You're trapped in a sinking car..."

2. **Indirect Questioning** - Asks "what happens?" not just "what is X?"
   - Not: "What is gangrene treatment?"
   - Instead: "What's your move?" (Cut off foot)

3. **Dark Humor** - Absurd but real scenarios
   - Throwing alligators at Wendy's
   - Radioactive health tonics
   - Dancing to death

4. **Educational Value** - All teach real facts
   - Water intoxication is real
   - Sucking snake venom doesn't work
   - Magnets can tear your intestines

5. **Clear Correct Answers** - No ambiguity
   - Every question has ONE objectively correct answer
   - All wrong answers are clearly wrong

6. **Host Commentary** - Snarky explanations
   - "Your brain swells and you're dead. Ironic."
   - "The 1920s were insane."
   - "Don't be bait."
