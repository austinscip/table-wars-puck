## SUMMARY OF CHANGES

**Total Games:** 41
**Safe Names (No Change):** 15 games
**Renamed Games:** 26 games

**Legal Basis:**
- Game mechanics ✅ CANNOT be copyrighted
- Specific names/branding ❌ CAN be trademarked
- Our approach: Same mechanics, 100% original names

**Example:**
- ❌ "Fibbage: Puck Lies" (Jackbox trademark)
- ✅ "Lie Detector" (generic name, same mechanics)

**All renamed games use:**
- Generic descriptive names (Spin to Win, Year Hunter)
- Common phrases (All or Nothing, Double or Nothing)
- Original creative names (Death Quiz, Screw the Crew)

**No Jackbox references** in any game name.
