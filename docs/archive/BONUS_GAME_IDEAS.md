## 游꿡 TIER 1: SINGLE-PUCK PARTY GAMES

### 1. 游꽄 CHUG TIMER

**Concept:** The puck IS your shot timer!

**How it works:**
- Puck starts pulsing green (GO!)
- Player picks up their drink
- Shake puck when you finish
- Faster chug = more LEDs light up
- Records your time

**Why it's GENIUS:**
- Ties directly into bar culture
- Creates instant competition
- Easy to film for social
- Scoreboard shows fastest chuggers
- Bar owners LOVE this (sells more drinks!)

**Scoring:**
- < 3 seconds: LEGEND (+1000)
- < 5 seconds: BEAST (+500)
- < 10 seconds: RESPECTABLE (+250)
- > 10 seconds: ROOKIE (+100)

### 2. 游 RANDOM CHALLENGE GENERATOR

**Concept:** Puck assigns YOU a random dare/challenge

**How it works:**
- Shake puck to randomize
- LED color reveals category:
  - 游댮 RED = Physical (pushups, dance, etc)
  - 游댯 BLUE = Mental (trivia, riddles)
  - 游리 YELLOW = Social (compliment, joke, story)
  - 游릭 GREEN = Drinking (sip, chug, buy round)
  - 游릮 PURPLE = Wild Card (group decides)
- Number of LEDs = difficulty level
- Tap to accept, hold for 3s to pass (penalty)

**Why it's GENIUS:**

- Keeps energy high
- Infinitely replayable
- Great for groups who just met
- Could integrate with an app later for actual challenges

### 3. 丘덢잺 SLAP BATTLE

**Concept:** Last person to slap the puck loses!

**How it works:**
- Puck sits in middle of table
- Counts down with pulsing (5-4-3-2-1)
- Random delay after countdown
- Suddenly goes GREEN + BEEP
- SLAP THE PUCK!
- Records reaction time
- Slowest person each round = eliminated

**Why it's GENIUS:**
- Physical comedy gold
- People will SLAM that puck
- Great spectator sport
- Could be 2-6 players taking turns
- Hilarious when drunk

**Scoring:**
- Fastest tap each round survives
- Last person standing wins
- Bonus points for < 100ms reactions

---

### 4. 游꿧 BEAT KEEPER

**Concept:** Can you keep a rhythm?

**How it works:**
- Puck plays a beat (beep-beep-beep at intervals)
- You must tap to continue the rhythm
- LEDs pulse with each beat
- Slowly speeds up
- Miss a beat = game over

**Why it's GENIUS:**
- Tests coordination
- Gets progressively harder
- Beautiful to watch (LEDs + rhythm)
- Could tie into bar music later
- Great for musicians/dancers

**Scoring:**
- Each successful beat: +10
- Bonus for perfect rhythm: +50/beat
- Speed multiplier as tempo increases

---

### 5. 游댠 PRESSURE COOKER

**Concept:** How long can you resist pressing the button?

**How it works:**
- DON'T press the button!
- Puck gets angrier over time:
  - 0-10s: Calm blue
  - 10-20s: Yellow + slow pulse
  - 20-30s: Orange + fast pulse + occasional vibrate
  - 30-40s: RED + constant pulse + frequent vibration
  - 40-50s: CHAOS MODE (strobing + beeping + vibrating)
- Press button = you lose (but earn points for time survived)
- Make it to 60s = LEGENDARY

**Why it's GENIUS:**
- Psychological torture
- Hilarious to watch people squirm
- Tests willpower
- Everyone yelling "DON'T PRESS IT!"
- Great viral content

**Scoring:**
- Score = seconds survived 칑 20
- 60s = +2000 bonus (LEGEND)
- Can only play once per session (too intense!)

## 游꿡 TIER 2: COMPETITIVE VARIATIONS

### 6. 游꿢 PRECISION TAP

**Concept:** Tap with EXACT timing

**How it works:**
- LED sweeps around ring
- Target zone is GREEN (1-2 LEDs wide)
- Rest of ring is RED
- Must tap when LED enters green zone
- Zone gets smaller each round

**Why it's GENIUS:**
- Tests precision vs speed
- Beautiful visual
- Satisfying when you nail it
- Different from LED Chase (precision vs chase)

---

### 7. 游깱 TSUNAMI

**Concept:** Shake to survive the waves!

**How it works:**
- "Wave" approaches (blue LEDs fill ring)
- Must shake HARD to "jump the wave"
- If you don't shake hard enough = wipeout
- Waves get bigger and closer together
- 10 waves total

**Why it's GENIUS:**
- Combines shake + timing
- Visual metaphor is clear
- Great audio (wave sounds via beeper)
- Could add surfing theme

---

### 8. 游꿣 TRIPLE SEVENS

**Concept:** Slot machine on a puck!

**How it works:**
- Three "reels" (sections of LED ring)
- Tap to stop each reel
- Match 3 colors = JACKPOT
- Match 2 colors = Small win
- Different color combos = different scores

**Why it's GENIUS:**
- Gambling mechanics
- Everyone understands slots
- Could pay out in drinks
- Visual is immediately clear

---

### 9. 游빋 DEFUSE THE BOMB

**Concept:** Button sequence to defuse (like bomb squad)

**How it works:**
- Random sequence: Tap-Hold-Shake-Tap-Tap
- Instructions shown via LED patterns
- Must complete in 15 seconds
- Sequence gets longer each round
- Wrong input = explosion

**Why it's GENIUS:**
- Multi-input gameplay
- Creates tension
- Tests memory + execution
- Could add "cut the wire" via shake direction

---

### 10. 游끢 ESCAPE ROOM

**Concept:** Solve a sequence of mini-puzzles

**How it works:**
- 5 random mini-challenges in sequence:
  - Tap 10 times in 2 seconds
  - Hold perfectly still for 3 seconds
  - Match the color (button when LED matches)
  - Shake to X intensity
  - Simon says (short pattern)
- Complete all 5 to "escape"
- Timer counts total time

**Why it's GENIUS:**
- Tests multiple skills
- Every playthrough different
- Escape room theme is HOT right now
- Speedrun potential
