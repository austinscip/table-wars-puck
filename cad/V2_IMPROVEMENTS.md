## What's Still Missing (Would Need Professional CAD)

**To get to TRUE Forerunner aesthetic:**
- Intricate surface glyphs and symbols
- Complex curved transitions
- Organic "ancient tech" sculpting
- Fine surface textures
- Custom light guide geometries
- Advanced surfacing techniques

**OpenSCAD limitations:**
- It's a programming-based CAD tool
- Great for functional/parametric design
- Not ideal for artistic/organic shapes
- Can't do complex NURBS surfaces
